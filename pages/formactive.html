During my work with Dewitt Godfrey, much experimentation has been done to find new ways of exploring behaviour driven geometry. As an artist he's always looking for new effects or means of feedback between the digital and physical. Majority of this work has been for various proposals that may or may not have been successful, and this page shows a selection of this "background work".

A continuous topic has been to capture more unpredictable behaviours in a digital process. This represents a computational equivalent to the artists early sculptures which were the product of more "informal" experimentation with rolled steel and its elastic behaviour.
